If you're here, you probably know the purpose of this page. My favourite tools are below.

## Hardware & Desk

- 💻 **MacBook Pro 14-inch M1 Max** – Time to upgrade soon!
- 📱 **iPhone 17 Pro Max** – I'm sure you've heard of it.
- 🔐 [**Omarchy PC**](https://omarchy.org/) **–** my primary dev setup is running Omarchy on a spare mini-PC. I connect to Opencode via Tailscale.
- ⌨️ [**Mode SixtyFive Keyboard**](https://modedesigns.com/products/sixtyfive) – my home office keyboard. I'm using Durock Silent Shrimp switches and [GMK R&D 1973 MTNU keycaps](https://www.norbauer.co/products/r-d-1973)
- ⌨️[**NuPhy Node 75 Keyboard**](https://nuphy.com/products/nuphy-node-series-high-profile?variant=43813526700141) – daily driver at the office. My only stock keyboard. I have it in high-profile in Lunar White with Blush Max switches.
- ⌨️ [**Keychron Q1 Max**](https://www.keychron.com/products/keychron-q1?variant=39951530885209)– backup home keyboard in Shell White. Using Gateron Nano Banana switches with [GMK Botanical CYL](https://omnitype.com/products/gmk-botanical-2-keycap-set) keycaps.
- 🍑 [Herman Miller Aeron Chair](https://www.hermanmiller.com/products/seating/office-chairs/aeron-chairs/) – This is my chair. There are many like it but this one is mine.
- 👨🏼‍💻 [Herman Miller Renew Sit-to-Stand Desk](https://www.hermanmiller.com/products/tables/sit-to-stand-tables/renew-sit-to-stand-tables/product-details/) – I have this one in walnut. Standing is a good idea when you remember to do it.
- **Grovemade Felt Desk Pad** – been enjoying the feeling of this desk pad over leather options.
- 🖥 **Apple Studio Display** – I have two of these in the NanoTexture finish.
- 💧 [Rain Design mStand](https://www.raindesigninc.com/mstand.html) – A perfect perch for my MacBook.

## Software

- ⌨️ [Cursor](https://cursor.com/)
- ⚡ [Opencode](https://opencode.ai/) \+ [**Nvim**](https://www.lazyvim.org/)
- 🔍[**Raycast**](https://www.raycast.com/) **–** for everything. Search, application launcher, window management, clipboard history. Raycast does it all.
- ⚡️ [**Ghostty**](https://ghostty.org/) – I use zsh. Can't decide if I prefer Ghostty or iTerm2 but keep Ghostty for consistency across OS.
- 🌐 [**Tailscale**](https://tailscale.com/) – to access my networked devices as if I was local, from anywhere.
- 🎡 [**Laravel Valet**](https://laravel.com/docs/12.x/valet) – If I'm working on a Wordpress site, I use Valet to park it.
- 😴 [Insomnia](https://insomnia.rest/) – For HTTP testing.
- 🔢 [Numi](https://numi.io/) - A running feed of my calculator. Probably one of my favourite apps.
- 🔑 [1Password](https://1password.com/) - Keeping me sane amongst all these generated passwords. Also manages 2FA for everything.
- 🌎 [NordVPN](https://www.tripmode.ch/) - My VPN of choice. Invaluable in airports and coffee shops.
